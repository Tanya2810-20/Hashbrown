Integrated OOH technology stack to plan, monitor, and manage your outdoor advertising campaigns.

The Hashbrown OOH Suite

The OOH suite by Hashbrown Systems is a complete set of tools & applications to efficiently and effectively manage and administer all aspects of an outdoor advertising and marketing campaign. The system appropriates Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Geospatial analytics to create a set of location intelligence tools that support planning, monitoring, auditing and provide valuable performance metrics, after and most importantly during the course of a campaign.

OOH Campaign Planning

The planning tool analyzes millions of data points and location clusters to build data-driven plans for outdoor advertising and branding campaigns.

OOH Monitoring & Audit

Integrated suite of applications that provide a complete view of your campaigns, ensure compliance, and provide real-time proof of performance.

Analytics, Impressions & Insights

Analyze campaign performance metrics using traffic data, impressions, reach, and on-ground activity insights.

Outdoor Media Campaign Planning

Our Media Planning platform is one of a kind marvel for all the stakeholders of Out-of-Home industry. It captures, classifies, segments and compartmentalizes millions of POIs into one persuasive and functional tool.

Pinpoint Premium Hotspots

Identify hotspots based on consumer cohorts, footfall density, POIs, and understand where your target audience is located.

Impressions, Reach & Analytics

Analyze estimated reach, impressions, audience behaviour, and target areas with the highest audience density and concentration.

Build Media Plans

Add media assets to your plan and visualize your entire campaign on an interactive map.

Audit, Monitoring & Management

Our audit and media monitoring software is an integrated suite of tools and applications built on our proprietary technology. The software is designed to monitor and manage large-scale OOH campaigns across multiple locations. Campaign performance is monitored through geo-tagged images, verified locations, and site condition remarks.

Out-of-Home Media Monitoring & Verification Services

We offer Independent Monitoring and Verification Services for Out-of-Home campaigns. Our field teams carry out on-ground site checks, capture geo-tagged, time-stamped photos, and record site condition and campaign status as it unfolds. We provide Excel & PDF reports, including proof of performance, campaign progress, and location metadata.

Analytics, Insights & Visualization

Our OOH suite integrates location intelligence and movement data to measure, classify, and visualize the impact of your OOH campaigns. For agencies and brands to analyze movement, exposure, and location performance through an objective, data-first approach.

Real Time Traffic Analysis

Measure, classify, and analyze vehicle and pedestrian circulation. Our software identifies high-level trends and seasonal patterns to help you gauge the true effectiveness and visibility of every outdoor media asset.

Location Analysis

Location analysis combines demographic data with millions of Points of Interest (POIs) to deliver a quantitative view of audience presence, behaviour, and surroundings. By evaluating site and area-level characteristics, the system identifies target cohorts and lookalike audiences, providing a clear understanding of your target audience.

Impressions & Cost Metrics

Assess your campaign performance with data-backed ROI calculations. Our software provides estimated impressions, reach, and cost metrics for individual sites or suggested clusters, making it easier to plan and run highly effective campaigns.

Performance Data & Visualization

Track campaign performance through intuitive charts and graphs. Visualize how your media is performing in real-time to see which locations are working best. The dashboard provides a clear campaign overview and ensures compliance across all media placements.

Build your own OOH tech

Build your own Outdoor Advertising Management Software that includes an array of tools and application for media & campaign planning, audit & monitoring, and reporting and analytics for the out-of-home industry.

Media Planning

Design your own data-driven planning tool that uses machine learning algorithms and location intelligence. Create precise and definitive media plans to optimize your advertising campaigns.

Monitoring and Execution

Develop mobile applications with tamper-proof mechanisms that provide real-time updates and verifications and deliver quantifiable results to ensuring 100 % compliance.

Road Traffic Analytics

Incorporate comprehensive solutions to measure, classify, and analyze road traffic. Utilize these insights for better planning, effective branding, and marketing attribution; these insights are a true indicator of trends, circulation, and ROI for your out-of-home media.

iStrat

iStrat is our proprietary pricing algorithm designed for outdoor media owners. It uses historical sales data, occupancy, seasonality, repeat usage, and audience context to suggest pricing and discount ranges. Recommendations adjust based on factors such as brand type, frequency, and demand patterns, helping sales teams respond to market conditions without relying on ad-hoc discounting.
